Detailed Description

The lid driven cavity experiment consists of a cavity Ω = [0, 1]² where the top lid is diven with a velocity v = 1. A detailed description of the experiment can be found in section 5.3 here. An interesting observation from this experiment is the behavoir of the algorithm when confronted with discontinuous dirichlet boundary conditions.
